Saving our Seas

With increasing use on plastic, our oceans are filled with plastic, hurting not only the organisms that live in it, but our own scarce water supplies.

What can we do to help?

A lot of pollution comes from single-use plastics, which leads to the deaths of many aquatic organisms, as well as poisoning our water supply with microplastics. So by using reusable bags and bottles, we can lower our necessity on plastic, and save thousands of aquatic lives.
