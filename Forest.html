The Fates of the Forest

Forests around the world are experiencing tremendous stress, with many forest ecosystems feeling its effects from increasing deforestation rates, to forest fires caused by the Climate Change.

What can we do to help?

Although we can't prevent deforestation, we can slow its constant increasing rates by being effecient with what we do buy. By relying less on paper products, and using them to the highest potential, we can work together as a community to lower paper usage, and thereby lower deforestation.
