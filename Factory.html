Reliable Replacements

One of the biggest contributing factors to the ever increasing issue of climate change are energy consumption. Oil and Coal release dangerous greenhouse gases as well as other pollutants that gravely harm the surrounding environment, and the world as a whole.

What Can We Do?

By switching to more reliable methods of energy, such as solar and geothermal energy, we can create a huge difference in climate change rates. Additionally, we can work as community to push these energy effecient methods to be more popular, convincing more individuals to go green.
